ABOUT
JEEV AVAM JAN KALYAN SEVA TRUST
your ngo in need

Founded by Mr. JAIPAL SINGH and two others in 2019, jeev avam jan kalyan seva Trust is a Registered Nonprofit Social Organization working for child development ,religious matters ,poverty and many other matters of deprieved socities across India. Since its inception it has been working towards creating a generation of children who are not bound by the circumstances they are born into. Coming from the most desperate of situations, often with alcoholic fathers and a distressed home environment, their future looks bleak. At this organization such children are provided with education, food, shelter, love, care, and guidance in an effort to help them enjoy a happy life. Kavitha, born and brought up in a town of amroha, lost her mother at a very young age. Raised by her relatives, her painful experiences of living without a mother’s love lead her to start jeev avam jan kalyan seva Trust. Through this organization deprieved section ,orphaned and abondened of societies are provided love, food, shelter, and education.

The Trust was established to respond to the widespread of alcoholic and other related problem in the nearby areas of gajraula of district amroha and has been singularly successful addressing this problem. Apart from primary healthcare, education, sustainable livelihoods, and advocacy have been the prime focus areas of Jeev avam jan kalyan seva Trust. With a prime objective of ‘Reaching the Unreached’ to provide healthcare to the underserved and the poor in the remote areas, Karuna Trust continues to serve the community it has been serving with renewed vigor and dedication.

OUR VISION

Our Vision is for a society in which we strive to provide an equitable and integrated model of health care, education and livelihoods by empowering marginalized people to be self reliant.

OUR MISSION

Our Mission is to develop a dedicated service minded team that enables holistic development of marginalized people, through innovative, replicable models with a passion for excellence.

“Reaching the Unreached”

JEEV AVAM JAN KALYAN SEVA TRUST reaches out to those
who are in the remote and inaccessible areas, hilly and tribal regions,
naxalite and insurgency areas with poor roads,
no phone and electrical connectivity, where both public and private
health services have hardly reached the poor.
